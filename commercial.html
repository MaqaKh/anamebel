Custom Solutions

We bring your ideas to life with solutions that are built to last.

For over 60 years, Justice has been manufacturing high-quality commercial furniture and mattress for hotels, resorts, senior living centers, and other facilities. Using domestically sourced materials, our experienced team of craftsmen design and build durable furniture—on-time and with competitive pricing—that can withstand high traffic and challenging environments.

From large-scale guest rooms to small public space areas, you can rest assured that we will be here for you every step of the way. We custom-build sofas, chairs, ottomans, headboards, benches, booths, banquettes, and your creations to your specifications, delivering on budget and on schedule.

Custom Solutions

Driven by Design

Our product development is driven by design. From the first idea in a designer’s imagination, right down to the details of how to create and manufacture, we understand the importance of working side-by-side with designers because their ideas are crucial to the success of a project. We ensure form, function, and comfort bringing your vision to life, without compromising quality and while maintaining design intent.

PROJECT OPTIONS: We know budgets are important to the bottom line. We provide value engineering and alternate material options if needed.

Custom Solutions

We Reinforce Your Brand and Fit Your Budget

HOSPITALITY
We have been designing and building custom, quality hospitality furniture and mattresses for hotels, resorts, and establishments for more than 60 years. You can rely on us to create comfortable, stylish, durable furniture for high-traffic environments in which your guests can relax and enjoy life.

SENIOR LIVING
Our designers and craftsmen are experts at creating accessible, safe, and robust senior living furniture for senior care facilities and other healthcare environments. Your patients and residents will find our furniture beautiful, strong, and comfortable.

Custom Solutions

We Build Relationships

At Justice, we believe in establishing long-term relationships with our clients. We take the time to learn about you and your needs, then work with you through each phase of the manufacturing process, from the initial design concept to completion. This customized, design-driven approach allows us to manufacture furniture that meets the individual needs and personality of each property. We are a partner in your success.

STANDARDS & SAFETY
Our furniture and mattresses are made to the utmost requirements with high-quality materials to meet Hospitality and Contract standards. Safety and durability are always at the forefront of our mission. We are committed to providing our hospitality partners with quality products that withstand the test of time.

Custom Capabilities

Justice is known for flexibility and service to create the perfect piece for a space. We can customize every element, working hand-in-hand with designers to tailor shapes, materials, finishes and technical specifications.

Our Lead-Time

We produce prototypes in-house for quicker review with opportunity to make timely adjustments. An efficient manufacturer, Justice turns production around in a shorter lead-time than import competitors.

USA Centrally Located

Justice is dedicated to maintaining accurate schedules for deliveries so you can meet deadlines. We have our own fleet and our factory is centrally located in the U.S., making logistics a fraction of the cost of overseas manufacturers.
