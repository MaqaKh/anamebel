Why Choose Justice

The Justice
Difference

The most important thing we build is our reputation. Our goal is to offer every customer premium designs, quality products and impeccable customer service. We are committed to building long-term relationships with our customers by listening to their needs and supplying solutions.

Justice Furniture is built for life...

What Matters is on the Inside

Kiln-dried hardwood frames, double-dowelled, glued and stapled with reinforced corner blocks. Independent drop-in coil spring system. Reinforced front, side, and back rails. Back cushions with quality Poly Fiber or High Resilient Polyurethane foam. Seat cushions are a combination of High-Density Polyurethane foam with a Dacron wrap for added comfort.

Handmade in Lebanon, MO

Proudly Crafted by American Hands

From our factory to your homes and businesses, our furniture is still designed, crafted, and built in the USA.

OUR PEOPLE are loyal, skilled, and passionate. We have team members that have been crafting furniture at Justice for decades. We maintain the tradition of handcrafting furniture through every stage of the production process: from sourcing American-made components to constructing the frames to cutting and sewing the fabric to final upholstering.

Quality materials

There’s no Substitute for Quality

Carefully forged over all these years, our close supplier relationships ensure we can source our materials from companies that parallel our devotion to the craft.

Discerning consumers recognize the difference when investing in quality. That difference? The most comfortable, durable, stylish furniture they will ever own.

Warranty

Confidence in Our Craftsmanship

We are so confident in our workmanship and materials that we offer a Lifetime Warranty on workmanship, frame, all springs, and seating foam.
